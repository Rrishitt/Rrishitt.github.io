Why Execution Breaks Down in Consumer Marketplaces (Zomato)

By Rishit Jain

Context

Zomato operates a high-frequency, two-sided marketplace where execution quality — not strategy — determines outcomes. Despite strong demand and brand recall, trust and delivery reliability remain inconsistent.

Problem Framing

The issue is not delays themselves. The real question is why execution remains inconsistent despite scale, data, and mature systems.

Insight

This is a decision-architecture problem, not a technology problem.

Recommendation

Shift from fastest-possible delivery to reliability-weighted execution through incentive redesign and expectation management.
